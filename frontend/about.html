About SecTech

Securing the future, one byte at a time.

The SecTech Logo shows a shield with a keyhole. The shield represents security, while the keyhole stands for accessibility. SecTech connects these two concepts by making security accessible to everyone.

The SecTech Logo is a registered trademark of SecTech GmbH. All rights reserved.

At SecTech, we believe that everyone deserves the right to privacy and security in the digital age. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality tools to protect your personal and professional data.

Whether you want to secure your home network or your business, we have the tools you need to keep your data safe. We strive for excellence in our products and services, and we are committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers.

The wordmark SecTech is a deliberately shortened and abstracted coined term derived from the words Security and Technology. In contrast to the logo, it does not show our mission, but our identity. We provide Technologies for Security, helping our customers to thrive in a secure environment.

The SecTech wordmark is a registered trademark of SecTech GmbH. All rights reserved.

SecTech GmbH was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate cybersecurity researchers and developers. We are based in Vienna, Austria. We are a small team of dedicated professionals who are incorporating the latest cybersecurity research into our products to deliver state-of-the-art security solutions.

We offer a variety of cybersecurity tools, including antivirus software, firewalls, and encryption solutions. We also provide training and support to help you get the most out of our products.

We also develop security software for clients on request. If you are interested in our services, please contact us via the link at the bottom of the page!

We use a combination of purple colors as a recurring brand element. The colors represent innovation, creativity, and a forward-looking mindset. We also use the color purple to distinguish our brand from competitors. The security sector predominantly uses black, grey, blue, or red tones. With purple, we create a new experience from the established standard.
